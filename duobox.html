Operating modes

Extended backup and grid services.

DuoPower manages grid-service operation under CERP rules, while your home receives backup reserve, bill savings, and CERP payments without buying the battery.

Grid service

Responds to grid dispatch commands during CERP contracted dispatch windows.

PSPS event watch

Suspends dispatch and pre-charges ahead of planned power outage events.

Whole home power backup during an emergency blackout from the available DuoBox state of charge under CERP terms.
